We are ParkHour

The ParkHour team comprises 4 members, Jake Ginobli, Benj Calum, Drake Powell, and Toby Guire.

As a professional parkour and specialist stunt performance team, they undertake work for movies, advertising, digital activations, live performances, event guesting and more. The collective is also available to hire as a self-sufficient film production unit for a wide range of projects.




Since 10.01.23

ParkHour was officially established on 1st October 2023. Although the guys have been already training a few years prior, this was the day the 4 friends decided to launch this brand and start doing Youtube Videos.

At first it was just a casual thing with them uploading videos of training sessions about parkour and sometimes just messing around and hanging out in their hometown. As the group began to travel more widely together, they documented their journey and adventures.

VIDEOS

ParkHour's videos offer a distinct viewpoint, delving into cities by navigating their rooftops, winding through back-alleys, and exploring urban gathering places while seamlessly traversing both man-made and natural settings. When combined with their exceptional visual blend of point-of-view shots, dynamic drone footage, and intimate filming by parkour-cameramen, the audience experiences an immersive sensation as if they are at the center of the action, setting their travelogue apart from any other.




Parkour

As part of the global parkour community, ParkHour effortlessly connects with the vibrant youth culture of any country they explore. Parkour, the rapidly expanding extreme sport, possesses a remarkable ability to transcend cultural and socioeconomic boundaries. Requiring no special gear or attire, it offers accessibility to individuals of all backgrounds. The team takes pleasure in introducing parkour to underprivileged children and teenagers in various communities worldwide.
